Public Release Date:  25 September 2014True colors: Female squid have two ways of changing color, according to a UCSB study

Researchers have discovered that the female market squid possesses a pair of rainbow iridescent stripes that flank a bright white stripe, which mimics the appearance of a male.

University of California - Santa Barbara
